Research

I like thinking about probability, entropy, thermodynamics and group actions in the context of machine learning. Also on modular forms (including half-integer weight) and their L-functions. My CV.
My Ph.D. advisor is Jeffrey Hoffstein from Brown University, and my postdoctoral mentor is Matthew Young from Texas A&M University. In Japan I was a JSPS postdoctoral fellow, working with Maki Nakasuji at Sophia University. I also was a SPDR fellow in RIKEN AIP where I made a switch to Machine Learning research.
